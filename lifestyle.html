Quick Kitchen Tips

Salt Pasta Water Add salt to boiling water before cooking pasta for better flavor absorption.
Room Temperature Ingredients Let eggs and butter reach room temperature for better baking results.
Sharp Knives A sharp knife is safer and more efficient than a dull one.
Heat Your Pan Preheat your pan before adding oil or ingredients for even cooking.
Rest Your Meat Let cooked meat rest 5-10 minutes before slicing to retain juices.
Acid Brightens Dishes A squeeze of lemon or splash of vinegar can elevate any dish.
Save Pasta Water Starchy pasta water is perfect for thinning and binding sauces.
Taste Before Serving Always taste your dish and adjust seasoning before plating.
Fresh Herbs Last Add delicate fresh herbs at the end of cooking to preserve flavor.
